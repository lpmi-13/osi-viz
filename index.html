One request, wrapped for delivery

Every request is a small piece of real data wrapped in layers of delivery metadata. Here you see all the layers at once: the bright core is your data; everything around it is envelope.

The bar shows the ratio — watch how little of what's on the wire is the thing you actually asked for. A GET or OPTIONS has no body at all, so it's pure envelope.

Tap any layer to see its real header fields, as tcpdump would show them. Use Prev / Next (or arrow keys) to watch the client wrap the request and the server unwrap it. Hit 🎲 New request for a fresh POST, GET, preflight and more.
