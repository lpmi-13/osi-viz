One request, wrapped for delivery

      
Follow a single request as it travels left → right. Each stop wraps it in one more header, so the packet grows a new coloured ring.

      
The bright core is the real data the app cares about. Watch it shrink to a sliver as delivery metadata piles on — that ratio is the whole idea.

      
Scroll or swipe to move the packet. Each node's colour is the ring it adds. Tap the packet to see its real headers, as a tool like tcpdump would show them.
